# Domain Vision Statement

## 1. The Problem
*For* marto-sh developers
*who* want to reliably enforce a precise development workflow.

## 2. The Solution
*The* libre-code
*is an* opinionated development harness
*that* reliably enforces processes instead of just documenting them.

## 3. The Differentiation
*Unlike* Claude Code, Codex, and OpenCode,
*our product* is built around a specific development process rather than trying to please everyone — overfitting on one workflow instead of underfitting on all of them.
