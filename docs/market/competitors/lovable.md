# Lovable

## Company Overview

Swedish startup, launched late 2024. Reached $100M ARR in ~8 months. $330M raised at $6.6B valuation (Dec 2025). ~8M users generating 100K+ projects per day. 50%+ of Fortune 500 employees using it.

## Product/Service Offering

AI app builder for non-developers (founders, designers). Converts natural language descriptions into web app MVPs. Targets rapid prototyping rather than production engineering.

## Pricing Strategy

Credit-based model. Free: 5 messages/day. Pro: ~$25/mo. Business: ~$50/mo.

## Marketing & Messaging

Positioned for non-technical builders who need functional prototypes fast. Emphasis on democratizing software creation.

## Market Perception

### Strengths

- Explosive growth ($100M ARR in 8 months)
- Large user base (~8M)
- Strong adoption among Fortune 500 employees

### Weaknesses

- Struggles with complex state management
- Barclays noted 40% traffic decline from peak
- Targets non-developers, limiting overlap with professional coding tools
