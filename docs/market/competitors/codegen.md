# Codegen

## Company Overview

Founded by Jay Hack. Raised $16.2M seed (Jul 2023) from Thrive Capital, Adam D'Angelo (Quora CEO), and Mike Krieger (Anthropic CPO). Valued at ~$60M. Acquired by ClickUp on Dec 23, 2025. Service deprecated Jan 16, 2026. Jay Hack joined ClickUp as Head of AI. No longer exists as a standalone product.

## Product/Service Offering

Ticket-to-PR automation integrating with GitHub, Linear, and Jira. Multi-agent orchestration: parent agent spawns children via sub-issues for parallel execution with isolation. Integration-first -- lived in project management tools rather than IDE. Strategic thesis: leverage is shifting from specialized agents to orchestration infrastructure.

## Pricing Strategy

No public pricing tiers before acquisition. Now integrated into ClickUp's pricing structure.

## Marketing & Messaging

"The SWE that never sleeps." Ticket-to-PR value proposition. Positioned around orchestration over individual agent capability. Integration-native: embedded inside tools teams already use.

## Market Perception

### Strengths

- Sophisticated investors validated the technology
- Defensible orchestration thesis
- Workflow-native integration (Linear/Jira)
- ClickUp acquisition validates the technology and approach

### Weaknesses

- No longer exists as a standalone product (deprecated Jan 2026)
- Underfunded ($16.2M vs $252M+ competitors)
- Narrow $60M exit
- Two-year lifespan from founding to acquisition
- No public benchmarks published
